Unit 1: Foundations of Curriculum

Lesson Overview

This unit introduces the meaning, concept, and nature of curriculum. Students will understand curriculum as a field of study, a plan for learning, and a system guiding educational practices.

Topics Covered

  • Definitions of Curriculum
  • Philosophical Foundations
  • Historical Development
  • Curriculum as a Discipline

Assignment

Answer the following questions:

  1. Explain the difference between curriculum as a plan and curriculum as a process.
  2. Discuss two philosophical foundations that influence curriculum development.
  3. Describe how curriculum has evolved historically in your education system.
